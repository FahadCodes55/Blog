Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit my own moral story?

We welcome story submissions! Please use the contact form above with "Story Submission" as the subject, and include your story in the message. We review all submissions carefully.

Can I use your stories for educational purposes?

Yes! Our stories are perfect for educational use. Teachers, parents, and educators are encouraged to share these stories to promote moral values and character development.

How often do you publish new stories?

We aim to publish new moral stories weekly. Subscribe to our newsletter to get notified when new content is available.

Are your stories suitable for children?

Most of our stories are family-friendly and suitable for children aged 8 and above. We clearly mark any content that might be more appropriate for older readers.
